How to Play

Objective: Eliminate all opponent's orbs to win.

Rules:

  • Turn-Based: Players take turns placing orbs on valid cells.
  • Placement: You can only place orbs on empty cells or cells you already own.
  • Explosions: When a cell reaches critical mass (4 orbs), it explodes!
  • Chain Reaction: Exploding orbs fly to adjacent cells, adding an orb and claiming them for you.
  • Grid: The game is played on a 5x5 grid.

Strategy Tip: Plan your chain reactions to wipe out the enemy!
